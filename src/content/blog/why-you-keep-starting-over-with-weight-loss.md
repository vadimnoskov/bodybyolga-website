You don’t have a discipline problem.

You have a reset problem.

You keep starting over.

Every Monday.
Every month.
Every “this time it’s different.”

And every time, you go all in.

* New plan
* Clean eating
* More workouts
* Less sugar

It works for a few days. Then life shows up.

Work runs late.
Kids need something.
You feel tired.

You eat off plan.

And your brain says: “I messed up.”

So what do you do? 

You reset. Again.

...

This is the cycle that keeps you stuck.

Not food. Not workouts.

**The reset.**

...

Every time you restart:

* you lose momentum
* you lose trust in yourself
* you go back to zero

And zero is exhausting.

...

Here’s the shift.

Stop restarting. Start continuing.

...

You overeat? Next meal is normal.

You miss a workout? Next day you train.

You feel off track? You stay in the game.

This is how consistency works.

Not perfect streaks.

Recovery speed.

...

The women who succeed don’t avoid mistakes.

They recover faster.

...

Ask yourself:

How fast do I get back on track?

That answer matters more than anything else.

If you fix this, everything changes.
